PhoneProfilesPlus Changelog

Version: 3.5.3

  • Renamed "Source profile" to "Shared profile" and moved as button into title of "Profile preferences".
  • Optimized battery consumption.
  • Prepared for Android O. Full support will be in next version.
  • Added support for MMS.
  • Renamed event sensor "SMS parameters" to "SMS/MMS parameters".
  • Fixed support for change of timezone. Please check event sensor "Time parameters" and fix start/end time. May be changed.
  • Added: 2 profile icons.
  • Appication language change in "Settings-Application interface-Language" is local for application (Android 6.0 and up).
  • Updated translations.
  • Bug fixes.
